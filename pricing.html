FAQs
What are the prices?
Our pricing plans are designed to fit various needs. We offer a free trial, followed by monthly and annual subscription options. Each plan comes packed with all features, ensuring you can create a standout resume or portfolio.
Are there discounts available?
Yes, we offer discounts for annual subscriptions and student plans. Additionally, promotional offers may be available during special events. Be sure to check our website regularly for the latest deals.
How is billing handled?
Billing is processed securely through our platform. You will receive an invoice via email upon subscription. You can manage your billing information directly from your account settings.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at anytime without penalties. Simply navigate to your account settings to initiate the cancellation process.
What payment methods accepted?
We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and ACH/PayPal. All transactions are encrypted for your security. For more details, please visit our payment information page.
